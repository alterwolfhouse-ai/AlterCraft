Cancellation Policy

AlterCraft accepts cancellation requests based on the project stage and product type.

  • Custom furniture can usually be cancelled before material purchase or fabrication begins.
  • Once cutting, finishing or custom procurement starts, cancellation may not be possible.
  • Rental or event furniture bookings can be rescheduled subject to availability.
  • Any approved refund depends on advance paid, work completed and material committed.

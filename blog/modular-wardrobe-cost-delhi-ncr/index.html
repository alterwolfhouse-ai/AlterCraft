Wardrobe Cost

Modular wardrobe cost in Delhi NCR: what changes the price.

A wardrobe quote changes with measurement, shutter type, board material, finish, internal layout, hardware and installation. This guide helps you compare quotes more carefully before choosing a vendor.

AlterCraft Guide Delhi NCR 8 min read

People often ask for wardrobe cost as one number. In reality, two wardrobes with the same width can have different budgets because the inside layout, shutter system and finish are different. A useful estimate should explain what is included, not only the final amount.

Size and height

Width, height and depth affect material use directly. A full-height wardrobe with lofts needs more board, more shutter area and more installation care than a smaller free-standing unit. Ceiling height and site conditions also matter.

Sliding or openable shutters

Openable wardrobes are usually simpler because they use hinge hardware. Sliding wardrobes save front clearance but need tracks, channels and better alignment. If the sliding hardware is poor, daily use suffers, so do not compare only the cheapest quote.

Material and finish

Board quality, laminate, acrylic, veneer, glass and mirror shutters all affect the budget. A practical laminate wardrobe can be very durable, while premium finishes raise visual value and cost. The right choice depends on use, room style and budget.

Internal storage

  • Drawers cost more than plain shelves but keep small items organised.
  • Loft storage adds material and shutter area but helps with low-use items.
  • Hanging space needs strong internal planning and proper rod placement.
  • Accessories, pull-outs and organisers improve use but increase the quote.
  • Mirror or glass shutters need safer handling and better fitting.

Installation and site work

Walls may not be perfectly straight, floors may slope and existing switchboards or beams may affect the wardrobe. A realistic quote should consider site measurement, delivery, installation and finishing responsibility.

AlterCraft tip

When comparing wardrobe quotes, ask what is included: material, shutter finish, internal layout, hardware brand or grade, lofts, handles, mirror/glass, delivery, installation and GST. A low number is not useful if half the scope is missing.

What to send for an estimate

Share wardrobe wall width, ceiling height, room photos, bed position, preferred shutter type, finish direction and a rough list of what you need to store. This helps create a more practical estimate before final measurement.

Quick answer

Modular wardrobe cost in Delhi NCR depends mainly on size, shutter type, material, finish, hardware, internal storage and installation conditions. Use online ranges as a starting point, but final pricing should be based on your actual wall and storage needs.

Wardrobe lighting and ventilation guide

Lighting and ventilation make a wardrobe easier to use and maintain. They are easiest to plan before fabrication because wiring, channels and sensor placement need space.

What to consider

  • Sensor lights for deep wardrobes and dressing areas.
  • Warm mirror lighting for dressing zones.
  • Ventilation gaps or suitable shutters in moisture-prone rooms.
  • Service access for drivers, wires and adapters.

A wardrobe should look clean from outside and still be practical to repair later.

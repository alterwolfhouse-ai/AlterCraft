Measurement Guide

How to measure a room for custom furniture

Good measurements make the first estimate more realistic. You do not need a professional drawing to begin, but a few details help a lot.

Measure these

  • Wall width, height and depth available.
  • Door and window positions.
  • Switchboards, sockets, AC points and curtain space.
  • Skirting height and floor level issues.
  • Clearance for shutters, drawers and walking space.

Take photos from all corners and one straight photo of the target wall. This helps the designer understand the room quickly.

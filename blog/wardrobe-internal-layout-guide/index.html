Layout basics

  • Keep daily-use shelves between waist and eye level.
  • Use drawers for items that get messy on open shelves.
  • Use lofts for luggage and rarely accessed storage.
  • Separate long hanging from short hanging.
  • Leave space for a laundry basket or safe if needed.

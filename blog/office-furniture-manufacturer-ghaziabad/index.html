What to plan before manufacturing

Before selecting laminates or desk shapes, define team count, departments, visitor movement, power points, storage needs, printer location, pantry relation and meeting space. This makes the furniture plan functional instead of decorative.

Common office furniture scope

Workstations

Team desks with cable access, modesty panels and storage where needed.

Reception

Client-facing counters, waiting area storage and brand wall coordination.

Cabins

Executive desks, back storage, credenzas and meeting corners.

Storage

Files, samples, supplies, pantry items and hidden utility storage.

Why site photos help

Office furniture depends heavily on room shape, columns, entry, windows, AC placement and electrical routing. Clear photos and rough dimensions help AlterCraft prepare a better first layout direction before final measurement.

Material and durability

Commercial spaces need surfaces that handle cleaning, daily use and cable movement. Laminates, edge treatment, hardware and under-table supports should be selected for use, not only appearance. Warranty support should be understood across eligible products, hardware and workmanship scope.

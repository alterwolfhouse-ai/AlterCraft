Storage Planning

Balcony storage cabinet design for Indian homes

Balconies often become storage zones for cleaning supplies, tools, shoes and seasonal items. A good cabinet keeps the balcony useful without making it look cluttered.

What to plan

  • Moisture exposure and rain direction.
  • Ventilation for cleaning liquids and utility items.
  • Raised base or legs if the balcony is washed often.
  • Shutter material that can handle dust and weather.
  • Separate shelves for tools, shoes and laundry supplies.

Outdoor or semi-outdoor furniture needs different material thinking from bedroom storage. Do not use a purely decorative finish where water and sun exposure are likely.

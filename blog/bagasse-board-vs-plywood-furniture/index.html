Plywood

Plywood is widely used for strength and moisture resistance, especially in kitchens, utility areas and heavy-use furniture.

How to choose

  • Use location and moisture exposure as the first filter.
  • Match the board to hardware load and shutter size.
  • Choose finish and edge banding carefully for longer life.

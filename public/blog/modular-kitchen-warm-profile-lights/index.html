Kitchen Lighting

Modular kitchen with warm profile lights

A kitchen planning guide for Ghaziabad, Noida and Delhi NCR homeowners.

Warm profile lights can make a modular kitchen look premium, but lighting should support cooking first. The best placement is usually under wall cabinets, inside selected tall storage, or along a clean ceiling/detail line where service access is possible.

Where warm profile lights work well

Under wall cabinets
Useful for countertop visibility during preparation and cooking.
Open shelves
Adds depth when the shelf is not overloaded with daily utensils.
Tall units
Helpful when lighting is serviceable and does not complicate hardware.

Do not ignore the practical side

  • Keep sink-zone material and edge banding moisture-aware.
  • Plan chimney, hob, fridge and microwave clearances before final design.
  • Use lighting where wires, drivers and repair access can be handled.
  • Choose warm white carefully; too yellow can distort cabinet colour.

Mandir Design

Pooja mandir design for small homes

A small home can still have a peaceful mandir if the design respects wall space, storage needs, lamp safety and daily cleaning.

Good small mandir ideas

  • Wall-mounted mandir with a compact drawer for essentials.
  • Vertical CNC back panel with warm LED lighting.
  • Foldable or shuttered mandir for multi-use rooms.
  • Corner mandir when the main wall is not available.

Keep enough depth for idols, diya placement and cleaning. If using lighting, plan wire concealment before fabrication.

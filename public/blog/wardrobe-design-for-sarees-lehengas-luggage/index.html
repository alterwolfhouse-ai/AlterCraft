Wardrobe storage guide

Wardrobe design for sarees, lehengas and luggage

Indian wardrobes need more than hanging space. Sarees, lehengas, luggage, blankets, jewellery, daily wear and seasonal clothes each need a different internal layout.

Suggested zones

  • Wide shelves for folded sarees and lehengas.
  • Loft storage for luggage and rarely used bedding.
  • Drawers for accessories, documents and smaller items.
  • Hanging space for shirts, dresses and occasion wear.
  • Mirror shutter or dressing niche if the room allows clearance.

Plan the internals first, then choose the shutter style and finish.

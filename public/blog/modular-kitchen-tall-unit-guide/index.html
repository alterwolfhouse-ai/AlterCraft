Kitchen Storage

Modular Kitchen Tall Unit Guide

Tall units help kitchens store more without cluttering counters. They work best when appliance height, pantry access and shutter swing are planned early.

What to decide first

  • Measure the available wall, floor clearance and service access before choosing the final design.
  • Match material and hardware to daily use, moisture exposure and maintenance expectations.
  • Plan storage around real behaviour instead of copying a catalogue image.
  • Keep wiring, lighting and future repair access visible in the design stage.

How AlterCraft approaches it

Common mistake to avoid

The most common mistake is choosing the look before checking clearances, storage needs and hardware quality. High-end furniture feels premium because it works every day, not only because it photographs well.

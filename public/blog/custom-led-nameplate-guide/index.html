Nameplate Design

Custom LED nameplate guide

A good nameplate should be readable, weather-aware and matched to the entrance. LED lighting adds visibility, but the design still needs clean typography and correct scale.

Design decisions

  • Choose a font that remains readable from the gate or corridor.
  • Use acrylic, wood-look, metal-look or layered materials based on exposure.
  • Plan warm or neutral LED based on wall colour.
  • Check wiring and adapter placement before installation.

For apartments, confirm society rules on size and lighting before fabrication.

Bedroom Storage

Storage bed buying guide for Indian homes

A storage bed is useful when the bedroom needs space for bedding, luggage and seasonal items. The design should be easy to open and strong enough for daily use.

Hydraulic vs box storage

Hydraulic beds offer easier access to a large storage area. Box storage can be simpler and cost-effective, but access may be less convenient depending on the shutter design.

What to check

  • Mattress size and room walking space.
  • Board strength and support frame.
  • Ventilation to avoid smell and moisture.
  • Headboard depth, switches and side table clearance.

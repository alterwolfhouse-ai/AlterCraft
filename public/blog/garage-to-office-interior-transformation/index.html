Garage to office interior transformation

A practical checklist for turning an unused garage or utility space into a small office.

A garage-to-office conversion can be a smart use of space, but it needs more technical thinking than a normal furniture project. The room may have ventilation issues, uneven flooring, weak lighting, exposed wiring, dampness or a shutter/entry condition that affects furniture placement.

Before planning furniture

  • Check ventilation, heat, dampness and water entry.
  • Confirm electrical safety, internet point and lighting needs.
  • Review flooring level and whether furniture bases need protection.
  • Decide workstation count, storage wall and visitor seating.
  • Plan acoustic comfort if calls or meetings will happen there.
Important: Furniture should not hide moisture, wiring or ventilation problems. The site condition should be reviewed before final quotation and production.

Interior Coordination

Coordinate false ceiling, wall panels and furniture before work starts

Many interiors look disconnected because ceiling, lighting, wall panels and furniture are designed separately. Coordination saves rework and makes the room feel intentional.

Plan together

  • Light points and panel highlights.
  • TV unit height and ceiling drop line.
  • AC, curtain and wardrobe clearance.
  • Material palette across ceiling, wall and furniture.
  • Service access for lights and wiring.

A room should have one visual language. CNC panels, TV units and lighting work best when they are planned as one composition.

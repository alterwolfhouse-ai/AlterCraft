Contractor Desk / Payment Gate

Contractor payment gate system: stop cash leakage before site work starts

Most contractor losses do not happen because the team cannot work. They happen because work starts before payment, material moves before money is clear, labour reaches site before scope is written, and the owner notices the loss only after the site is already emotional.

Contractor Desk treats payment as a gate, not as a polite reminder.

The problem with ordinary contractor tracking

A spreadsheet can show project value, but it rarely stops a risky action. A WhatsApp chat can prove a conversation, but it cannot warn the operator that a site should not move. A contractor needs a simple rule visible before every order, purchase and labour deployment: has the required payment stage cleared?

Advance gate

No work order moves until the first agreed advance is received and recorded.

Material gate

Material procurement starts only after the material amount is collected or approved in writing.

Labour gate

Labour is deployed only when site readiness and labour advance are visible.

Milestone gate

Production, installation and handover stages are linked to documented payment milestones.

How Contractor Desk solves it

The mobile app shows active jobs, pending payment, blocked sites and next action in one place. Instead of relying on memory, the operator sees whether a job is gate cleared, advance pending, cash hold or ready for next execution step.

  • Each job has a total value, received amount and pending amount.
  • The payment gate appears before job movement, material movement and site activity.
  • Blocked sites stay visible instead of being buried in chats.
  • Daily reports and proof are tied to the job before handover.

Best use cases

This system is useful for modular kitchen contractors, interior execution teams, furniture manufacturers, office interior vendors, site supervisors, civil vendors and renovation contractors who handle multiple small and medium sites.

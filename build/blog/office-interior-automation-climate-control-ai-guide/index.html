Smart office interiors

Office interior automation and climate control: what to plan first

Smart office planning works best when automation is designed with the furniture layout, not added after installation. Lighting, AC zones, blinds, meeting rooms and workstations all need clear wiring and service access.

Useful automation zones

  • Presence-based lighting for cabins, meeting rooms and pantry areas.
  • Climate control zones that avoid overcooling empty spaces.
  • Cable-managed desks and panels for clean workstations.
  • Sensor-ready storage and concealed access panels for maintenance.

Where AI helps

AI can help monitor usage patterns, draft maintenance alerts and suggest energy-saving schedules. It should support the operator rather than hide manual controls.

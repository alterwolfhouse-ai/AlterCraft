Start with size, but do not stop there

Most customers begin with a wall size, and that is correct. Width, height and depth define the basic cabinet volume. But a six-foot wardrobe with plain shelves is very different from a six-foot wardrobe with drawers, mirror shutter, loft, hanging zones and premium hardware.

Before requesting a quote, note wall width, room height, bed position, door swing, switchboard location and whether loft storage is required.

Sliding vs hinged wardrobe cost

Sliding wardrobe

Better for compact bedrooms where shutters cannot open freely. Track quality, shutter weight and maintenance access affect the quote.

Hinged wardrobe

Often simpler to build and maintain. It gives full access, but needs enough room for shutter opening.

Do not choose sliding only because it looks modern. Choose it when it solves a clearance problem. Do not choose hinged only because it may be simpler. Choose it when the room can support full shutter swing.

Internal layout is where the real value sits

A wardrobe should be planned around how your household stores clothes. Sarees, lehengas, shirts, trousers, daily wear, blankets, luggage, jewellery and documents need different zones. If the internals are wrong, even a beautiful wardrobe feels frustrating.

  • Wide shelves for folded sarees and occasion wear.
  • Hanging zones for shirts, dresses and coats.
  • Drawers for accessories, watches, documents and smaller items.
  • Loft storage for luggage, quilts and seasonal storage.
  • Mirror or dressing niche only where movement clearance allows it.

Finish and material choices

Laminate is practical for daily bedroom use. Acrylic adds gloss but needs careful cleaning. Veneer brings a warmer premium feel but should be used thoughtfully. Mirror shutters can make a small room feel larger, but they also need safe placement and cleaning consideration.

Useful rule: choose the internal layout first, then shutter type, then finish. A wardrobe that stores badly will not become useful because the outside looks premium.

Questions to ask before finalizing a wardrobe quote

  • What board/material is used for carcass and shutters?
  • How many shelves, drawers and hanging sections are included?
  • Is loft storage included?
  • Which hardware is included?
  • Are handles, mirror, lights or accessories separate?
  • Does the quote include delivery, installation and finishing?

Frequently asked questions

Can AlterCraft estimate wardrobe cost from photos?

Yes, an initial direction is possible from photos, wall size and storage needs. Final pricing should be confirmed after measurement and material selection.

Which wardrobe is best for a small Ghaziabad bedroom?

Sliding wardrobes are often better when bed clearance is tight. Hinged wardrobes are better when full access and simple maintenance matter more.

What makes wardrobe cost increase?

More drawers, lofts, mirror shutters, premium finish, heavy-duty hardware, lighting and complex site conditions can increase the quote.

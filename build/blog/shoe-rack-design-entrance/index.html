Design ideas

  • Slatted or ventilated shutters to reduce odour.
  • A small seating ledge if the entrance has depth.
  • Separate daily-use and occasional-use shelves.
  • Raised base for sweeping and mopping.
  • Top surface for keys, bags or decor.

For apartments, keep the shoe rack within society rules and avoid blocking corridor movement.

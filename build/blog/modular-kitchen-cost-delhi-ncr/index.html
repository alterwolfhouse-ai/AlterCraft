Kitchen Cost Guide

What changes modular kitchen cost in Delhi NCR?

Kitchen cost is shaped by layout, material, shutter finish, hardware, countertop, accessories and site work. A simple straight kitchen costs differently from an L-shaped or U-shaped kitchen with tall storage and appliance units.

Cost drivers

  • Layout: straight, L-shaped, parallel or island kitchen.
  • Cabinet material: engineered board, plywood, HDHMR or other options based on moisture exposure.
  • Finish: laminate, acrylic, membrane, PU or veneer.
  • Hardware: hinges, channels, tandem boxes, lift-ups and corner units.
  • Countertop: granite, quartz or solid surface choices.

Before asking for a quote, note wall length, ceiling height, appliance list, gas point, sink point and electrical points. Good planning reduces later changes.

Privacy Policy

Altercraft collects customer information only to respond to enquiries, share quotations, confirm orders, support online payments, manage deliveries, and provide after-sales communication. This includes information shared on the website, on WhatsApp, over calls, by email, or during payment and order confirmation.

Information We Collect

  • Name, phone number, email address, and delivery or site address shared by the customer.
  • Project or product requirement details, room dimensions, photos, and preference notes.
  • Payment-related confirmation data required to process order bookings or milestone payments.

How We Use It

  • To answer enquiries, create quotations, confirm products or services, and coordinate execution.
  • To support payment verification, order confirmation, invoicing, delivery, and customer service.
  • To improve product, pricing, and service communication on the website.

Data Sharing

Altercraft does not sell customer information. Data may be shared only with service providers or logistics/payment partners where required to process orders, verify payments, support delivery, or meet legal obligations.
